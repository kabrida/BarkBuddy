Welcome to BarkBuddy

BarkBuddy is a comprehensive dog management application that simplifies the process of keeping track of your beloved pets. Whether you are a proud dog owner or a pet enthusiast, this application is designed to meet your needs and help you manage your dogs effectively.

Features:

Our application offers a variety of features to enhance your experience:

  • Add Dogs: Easily add your dogs to the application by filling in their details such as name, date of birth, registration number, and breed. Keeping all this information in one place makes it convenient for you to manage your pet's profile.
  • View Dogs: View a comprehensive list of all the dogs added to the BarkBuddy application. This feature allows you to quickly access essential information about each dog, including their owners, making it easy to learn about various furry friends in the community.
  • Breed Info: Our breed information feature lets you search for specific dog breeds. By entering the breed name, you can retrieve detailed information and images from an external API, The Dog API. We extend our gratitude to The Dog API for providing reliable and comprehensive data, making it easy to learn about various breeds and understand their characteristics, helping you make informed decisions about pet ownership.
  • Edit Owners: For those with administrative access, you have the ability to manage dog owners' information. This feature allows you to update details about the owners, ensuring that all records are accurate and up-to-date.

Why BarkBuddy?

In today's fast-paced world, pet owners often find it challenging to manage their pets' information effectively. BarkBuddy addresses this issue by providing a user-friendly platform where you can store and retrieve important details about your dogs with ease. Our goal is to enhance your experience as a dog owner and promote responsible pet care.

Join our community of dog lovers and let BarkBuddy help you provide the best care for your furry companions!
