Why BarkBuddy?

BarkBuddy is designed to simplify your life as a pet owner. From adding and viewing dog profiles to finding breed information, our platform offers everything you need to manage your furry friends’ details in one place.

Features at a Glance

  • Add Dogs: Maintain a comprehensive profile for each dog.
  • View Dogs: Access profiles to learn more about your and others’ pets.
  • Breed Info: Get detailed breed information to support informed pet care.
  • Manage Owners: Admins can edit owner information, ensuring up-to-date records.
